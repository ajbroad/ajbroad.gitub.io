The Name

Factor Tree is a mathematical technique which objective is simplify or rewrite an expression in its fundamental terms. That is what we are about, we search for the essential avoiding excess.

Who are we?

The Company

Factor Tree is an ethical, vegetarian men footwear. We create limited timeless collections inspired on the traditional styles, emphasizing construction techniques and the quality of the materials used. We offer comfortable, versatility and lasting footwear for an urban active life.

Made in Spain

We design and manufacture in small workshops in Spain. Our espadrilles are made in limited editions by expert artisans taking care of each detail. The proximity to the factories makes as develop a close relationship which ensure integrity in the production, quality of the final product and reduce our carbon footprint.

Ecological

Our materials are resourced by the highest quality suppliers with certification. Produced in a responsible way, responding to ethical and ecological standards. Soft and gently with your skin, free of harmful chemicals and metals. Minimal use of dying, our goal is to preserve the beauty of authentic materials in its more pure stage.

Vegan

We believe that beautiful things cannot be created from the harm of an animal. All our components are entirely animal-free. We use natural fibres and technical materials with high performance features that respect people, animals and the planet.
