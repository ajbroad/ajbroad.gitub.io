Who are we?

Factor Tree is a mathematical technique used to simplify an expression down to its fundamental terms. This defines our goal - to search for the essential, avoiding excess.

We are an ethical men's footwear label with the goal of offering comfortable, versatile and lasting footwear for an active lifestyle. We create timeless pieces inspired by the traditional styles, emphasizing construction techniques and the quality of the materials.

Beneficial to your health

The uppers are made from the finest organic cotton and are free of harmful substances. This cotton is soft and gentle on your skin, hypoallergenic, breathable, anti-static and assists in neutralising UV radiation. The soles are made from yute, a 100% natural and ecological fibre which is extremely breathable and durable to keep your skin fresh and dry.

Ecological

We only use high grade materials, produced in a responsible way and responding to ethical and ecological standards. Our objective is to preserve the beauty of authentic materials in their purest form. We use organic cotton grown without pesticides or herbicides that comes with the OCCGUARANTEE. The cotton is free of inks and is in a natural ecru colour just like the plant.

Hand Made in Spain

We design and manufacture in small workshops in Spain. Our espadrilles are made in limited editions by expert artisans who take care of each detail like in the old days. The proximity to the factories enables us to develop a close relationship which ensures integrity in the production, quality of the final product and reduces our carbon footprint.

Vegan

We believe that beautiful things cannot be created from the harming of an animal. All of our components are entirely animal-free. We use natural fibres and technical materials with high performance features that respect people, animals and the planet.

Composition

  • Upper and lining made from organic cotton and free of inks
  • Ecological and breathable yute sole, hand stitched with care and dedication by expert artisans
  • Heel label made from ecological, vegan and breathable leather
