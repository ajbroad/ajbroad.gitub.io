Who are we?

Factor Tree is an ethical men´s footwear brand that is founded with the objective of delivering a product that is comfortable, versatile and lasting for an active lifestyle.

Offering timeless essentials, our designs are inspired by the traditional styles, emphasizing construction technique and the use of maximum quality materials.

Beneficial to your health

The uppers are made from the finest organic cotton and are free of harmful substances. This cotton is soft and gentle on your skin, hypoallergenic, breathable, anti-static and assists in neutralising UV radiation. The soles are made from jute, a 100% natural and ecological fibre which is extremely breathable and durable to keep your skin fresh and dry.

Ecological

We only use high grade materials, produced in a responsible way and responding to ethical and ecological standards. Our objective is to preserve the beauty of authentic materials in their purest form. We use organic cotton grown without pesticides or herbicides that comes with the OCCGUARANTEE. The cotton is free of inks and is in a natural ecru colour just like the plant.

Hand Made in Spain

We design and manufacture in small workshops in Spain. Our espadrilles are made in limited editions by expert artisans who take care of each detail like in the old days. The close relationship developed by the proximity to the factories assure honesty in the production, highest quality of the final product and minimises our environmental carbon footprint.

Vegan

We believe that beautiful things cannot be created from the harming of an animal. All of our components are entirely animal-free. We use natural fibres and technical materials with high performance features that respect people, animals and the planet.

Composition

  1. Upper and lining: organic cotton (OCCGUARANTEE) free of inks
  2. Hand stitched with care and dedication by expert artisans
  3. Vulcanized rubber to reinforce the sole (to make it durable)
  4. Jute sole. Breathable and ecological
  5. Heel label. Vegan, ecological and breathable leather (OEKO-TEX certified)
